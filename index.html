Environmental Data Research

Sharing environmental research is critical. That's why I have partnered with students to create OneAU, a place to share open-source projects answering questions about our world and the policies that govern it. Our work includes climate and atmospheric changes, the tracking of near-earth objects, environmental policy, and so much more.
Our contributors are students and professionals, all with a personal interest in better understanding the rock we call home. We aim to expand knowledge of these topics by presenting our results in a compelling, freely accessible way. Everything published to OneAU is an open-source project. Data comes from public sources, and the analysis produced is open to the world.
You can learn more and contribute at OneAU
